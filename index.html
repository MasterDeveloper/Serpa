Every brand is a journey & Sherpa is here to guide you.

Sherpa Creative is an independent design group that delivers captivating brand solutions. Offering a strategic approach to design, Sherpa focuses on powerful, on-target creative while executing on promised quality work and dedicated client service.

Business success follows a powerful brand & informed design.

We are a collaborative team, specializing in identity brand development, business collateral, online creative direction, event/promotional marketing, and indoor/outdoor signage. We serve a range of clientele within, but not limited to entertainment, financial, publishing, arts & culture, healthcare and non-profit sectors.
